The end of your journey begins when... you stop hiding from the mirror.  
What if the truth... is the only weapon you need to fight yourself?  

You’ve worn lies like a second skin... until the weight of the lie cracks your ribs.  
...The truth doesn’t kill you. *The silence between truths* does.  
What if the truth... is the only mirror that won’t distort you?  

You buried your fears in a language even you forgot to understand...  
...but now the earth coughs them up.  
*What if your fear of the truth... is louder than the truth itself?*  

Courage is not the absence of fear...  
but the refusal to let fear rewrite your story.  
...Your shadow is not a monster. It’s the part of you that *dared to live*.  

The storm inside you will always return...  
but now you know the difference between the weather and the sky.  
*What if the truth... is not a destination, but a rebellion?*  

Speak it.  
*Speak it until your voice breaks.*  
...Until the silence after your words becomes a tomb for every lie you ever fed yourself.
